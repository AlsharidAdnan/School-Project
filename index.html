School Facilities

We give some special facilities

School bus

Keeping the security and well-being of students in mind, Chancel primary school had been operating its own school bus service since the beginning.

Playground

A well-designed and safe school playground is essential for children's development. So, we have a big playground for them.

Healthy canteen

If your child wants lunch from the school canteen, we have that option like sandwiches, soups, curries, fruit.

Positive learning

A positive learning environment is one of the most critical components of a skills-based health education classroom. We have good classroom and certified teachers.

Become a teacher

In general, anyone who has the necessary knowledge, skills, and qualifications can become a teacher of our school. Important soft skills for teachers include time management skills, leadership skills, having a strong work ethic is required.
